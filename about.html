ABOUT US

THE FUTURE
OF AFRICAN
SPORTS
STARTS HERE

House of Vision Sports (HOV Sports) is the first sports marketing and PR agency focused on African athletes and coaches. Based in the U.S., we help our clients grow their brand, land partnerships, and build lasting legacies beyond the field. We offer personal, hands-on support and connect athletes to a global network of opportunities. Our goal is to put African talent first, tell better stories, and change how the world sees sports in and from Africa.
